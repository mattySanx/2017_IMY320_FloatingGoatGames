We The People is a 3D platform game designed to test your espionage skills. Set during the peak of the cold war, the game tells the story of a small band of hackers and defact spies from all over world who have one singular belief - that nuclear war will be the end of human kind. The player will assume the role of one of the agents and will infiltrate a new nuclear facility at each level to try and disarm the missiles.

Agent #3512

Country of origin:

America

Recruitment:

After spending years fighting in the civil rights movement group, the Black Panthers, Agent #3512 sought out our group as a means to bring down the ever impending threat of nuclear warfare rhetoric emerging from her home country. Skilled in espionage and hand to hand combat, Agent #3512 is one of our best and most loyal recruits.

Mission:

Infiltrate the nuclear facility located in Oak Ridge, Tennessee

Agent #1122

Country of origin:

Soviet Union

Recruitment:

Agent #1122 is a defected agent of the KGB. All those years surviving the extremist behaviour of the KGB forced Agent #1122 to find refuge here in our establishment. He brings the best to the table when it comes to technological hacking and intelligence gathering.

Mission:

Infiltrate the Semipalatinsk Test Site

Agent #8765

Country of origin:

Cuba

Recruitment:

Forced to leave Cuba during the Cuban Revolution, Agent #8765 received training in America under the CIA. Unhappy about the conditions and experimentation he found there, he sought after a more promising future with us here. He excels at long range shooting and intellegence gathering.

Mission:

Investigate missile sitings at San Cristóbal, Pinar del Río Province.
